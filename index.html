Telescopic Acrylic Magnetic Mudguard (TAMM)

For steel frame purists.

Forget about ugly mudguards. The Spups TAMM can easily fit in your backpack and be expanded when the rain comes, which is a lot in Scotland. It attaches using SS Neodymium magnets to your pristine steel frame.

Still in development but with working prototype. The acrylic is easily cleaned for those with OCD.

Detachable Brutto Cleat Straps

Brutto cleat straps for bike/hike or bike/ski mountineer.

Forget about carrying two pairs of shoes when you are hiking and cycling. Now you can use a single pair with detachable cleats.

We have a choice of a split strap for ski boots or standard strap for hillwalkers / hill walking boots.

On average, using detachable cleats will mean a 666g weight saving and 4 litre volume saving in your backpack, as you won't need to take SPD shoes AND hiking boots.
